NFL DRAFT RULES
The NFL Draft order is determined by the reverse order of each teams finish that season.
The teams that do not qualify for the playoffs are assigned picks 1 through 18.

The only tiebreaker for picks 1-18 is Strength of Schedule, weaker schedule gets you a higher pick.

Teams reaching the playoffs are assigned picks 19 through 32 based on their postseason finish.
The six teams eliminated in the Wild Card round will be assigned picks 19-24 based on regular season record.
The four teams eliminated in the Divisional round will be assigned picks 25-28 based on regular season record.
The two teams eliminated in the Conference Championships will be assigned picks 29 and 30 based on regular season record.
The team that loses the Super Bowl will be assigned pick 31.
The Super Bowl champion will be assigned pick 32.
